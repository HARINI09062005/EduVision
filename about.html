About EduVision

EduVision is dedicated to making technology accessible to everyone, with a special focus on empowering visually impaired individuals through innovative solutions like our Braille Bluetooth keyboard.

Our Vision

Accessible tech for everyone.

We believe that technology should be accessible to all, regardless of physical abilities. Our mission is to break down barriers and create inclusive digital experiences that empower everyone to participate fully in the digital world.

Our Team

Harini

Founder & CEO

Our Journey

2024

The Beginning

Harini founded EduVision after identifying a gap in accessible technology for visually impaired users.

2025

First Prototype

Development of the first Braille keyboard prototype and initial user testing with visually impaired participants.

2026

Expanding the Team

Michael Chen and Priya Patel joined the team, bringing expertise in development and accessibility.

2026

Beta Launch

Limited beta release of the EduVision Braille keyboard to select educational institutions and accessibility organizations.

2027

Public Launch

Official public launch of the EduVision platform and Braille keyboard, reaching thousands of users worldwide.

2027

Expanding Horizons

Development of additional accessibility tools and expansion into new markets, with a focus on educational technology.
