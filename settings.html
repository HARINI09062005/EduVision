Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate the microphone?

To activate the microphone, click on the microphone icon in the accessibility controls at the bottom of the page, or press Alt+M on your keyboard. You can then use voice commands to navigate the application.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the User Profile section and click on the "Change Password" button. You'll be prompted to enter your current password and then your new password twice for confirmation.

How do I enable high contrast mode?

To enable high contrast mode, go to the App Preferences section and select "High Contrast" from the Theme options. This will increase the contrast between text and background colors for better visibility.

How do I adjust the font size?

To adjust the font size, go to the App Preferences section and select your preferred size from the Font Size options (Small, Medium, or Large). You can also use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Plus to increase or Ctrl+Minus to decrease the font size.

How do I change the language?

To change the language, go to the App Preferences section and select your preferred language from the Language dropdown menu. The interface will update immediately to reflect your selection.
